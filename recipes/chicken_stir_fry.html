Chicken Stir-Fry

Description

A quick and healthy meal, this chicken stir-fry is packed with flavor and vegetables. The tender chicken and crisp vegetables are coated in a delicious sauce, making it a satisfying and satisfying dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast, sliced
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine chicken, soy sauce, and cornstarch. Marinate for 15 minutes.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat.
  3. Add chicken and cook until browned and cooked through.
  4. Remove chicken from skillet and set aside.
  5. Add onion, green bell pepper, and red bell pepper to skillet. Cook until softened.
  6. Stir in garlic, chicken broth, sesame oil, ginger, and red pepper flakes.
  7. Return chicken to skillet and cook until heated through and sauce has thickened.
  8. Serve immediately over rice or noodles.
